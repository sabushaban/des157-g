Audience Research

Name: Mary Miller

Age:16

Bio:

Mary Miller is a 16-year-old girl who aspires to be an artist. She doesn't know what medium she wants to focus in and wants to explore her possibilities. It is summer break and she tends to spend time mainly on her computer browsing the web trying to find inspirations from other artists. She has found is difficult to narrow down her interest, but she is fascinates by the possibilities we all hold to become artists in our own way.
